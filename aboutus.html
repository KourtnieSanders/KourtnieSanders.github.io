Just a Few Important Tips

Included below are a few tips that we feel are important for our clients to know.

What About WordPress?

Wordpress is one incredible tool! That's not to discredit the power of non-wordpress website. Wordpress however is an excellent option for a website owner who is looking to manage their content with a ease.

Don't Forget Functionality!

During your preliminary consultation make the functionality of the website a top priority vs. the websites asthetics. The most costly alterations are alterations which involve changes to the functionality of the website.

How to Ease Vlogging Shyness

If anxiety is stoping you from recording your first vlog, may we suggest that you get in a good headspace prior to recording. Listen to music, watch a comedy skit, call someone you love, etc. Ease your anxiety before you record, and recording should be a breeze.

Avoid Breaking Copyright Rules

Check to make sure you have the legal right to use the images or font that you are going to utilize to permanently represent your business or web project. A brand is very important and it shouldn't be taken lightly.
